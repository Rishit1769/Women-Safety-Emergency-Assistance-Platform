Core Design Philosophy

RakshaAI’s UI/UX must feel:

Calm during normal usage
Urgent during emergencies
Trustworthy at all times
Fast and intuitive under panic conditions

The interface should balance:

Emotional reassurance
Operational clarity
Real-time responsiveness
Minimal cognitive load

🧠 18. UX for Panic Situations
Panic-Centered UX Rules

During emergencies:

Reduce text
Reduce decisions
Increase button size
Prioritize clarity
Use reassuring messaging
Example Emergency Copy

❌ BAD:

“Emergency protocol sequence initiated.”

✅ GOOD:

“Help is coming.”

That distinction matters enormously under stress.
