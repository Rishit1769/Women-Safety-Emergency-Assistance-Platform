# 📌 1. Product Information

## Product Name

# **RakshaAI**

### *AI-Powered Women Safety & Emergency Response Ecosystem*

---

## Product Type

* Web-Based Platform
* Mobile Application (Flutter)
* Real-Time Emergency Assistance System

---

## Product Vision

RakshaAI aims to create a scalable AI-powered emergency response ecosystem that helps women instantly access emergency assistance, share live locations, connect with verified responders, and receive intelligent safety guidance during dangerous situations.

The platform combines:

✅ Real-Time SOS System
✅ AI Risk Prediction
✅ Smart Journey Monitoring
✅ Community Safety Intelligence
✅ Police & Volunteer Coordination
✅ Live Emergency Tracking

to improve personal safety and reduce emergency response time.

---

# 🚨 2. Problem Statement

Women frequently face unsafe situations while:

* Traveling alone
* Working late hours
* Using public transportation
* Living independently
* Navigating unfamiliar areas

---

## Current Challenges

Existing safety systems suffer from:

❌ Delayed emergency response
❌ Dependency on manual phone calls
❌ Lack of nearby responder coordination
❌ Poor real-time tracking
❌ Limited community safety visibility
❌ No predictive safety intelligence

Additionally:

* Victims often panic during emergencies
* Unsafe areas remain unidentified
* Authorities lack centralized incident visibility
* Existing systems are reactive instead of proactive

Humanity somehow built food delivery systems that track a samosa in real time but still struggles with rapid emergency protection infrastructure. Remarkable species.
